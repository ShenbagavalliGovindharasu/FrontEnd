Careers in Travel and Tourism

       A career in travel and tourism can be exciting and challenging. Training to become a travel agent varies, but travel experience can help any candidate, as well as experience or training in the tourism industry. Other careers in travel and tourism include becoming a flight attendant, filmmaker, or teacher. Making a career based on travelling is possible and there are various options for creating such careers, or even for creating your own travel agency.
